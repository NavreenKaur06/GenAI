Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI)
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that develops systems capable of performing tasks requiring human intelligence such as learning, reasoning, decision-making, language understanding and image recognition.

Applications of AI
• Virtual Assistants
• Recommendation Systems
• Healthcare
• Autonomous Vehicles
• Fraud Detection

Types of AI
• Narrow AI
• General AI
• Super AI

What is Generative AI?
Generative AI creates new content such as text, images, audio, video and code using trained AI models.

AI Workflow
Input Data → Processing → Model Training → Prediction/Generation → Output
